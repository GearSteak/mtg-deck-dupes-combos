Welcome to Card Checker

          
Here’s the quickest way to get your decks, collection, and shared cards organized.

        

      


      
    
        
  1. Import your collection and decks.
    On Imports & Decks, upload a ManaBox/Deckstats CSV, import public Deckstats decks, or paste lists manually.
    2. 
        
  2. Analyze your lists.
    Analyze finds shared cards, Commander Spellbook combos, and cards missing from your collection. Quick assign skips the slower combo lookup.
    3. 
        
  3. Choose what to compare.
    Shared & Locations lets you select specific decks, then record which sleeved deck currently holds each physical staple. Use the Lands tab for basic land pools (multi-copy).
    4. 
        
  4. Get a deck ready to play.
    Play a Deck tells you what staples and basics to pull from bulk or another deck before game night.
    5. 
        
  5. Check the other tabs.
    Use Combos for known interactions, Owned / Missing for buy and sleeve lists, and Collection to search what you own.
    6.
